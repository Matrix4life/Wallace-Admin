Services

Everything that pulls you away from your clients.

I handle all of the non-clinical operations of your practice — from the first inquiry to the final invoice — so you can stay in the room with your clients.

Scheduling & Calendar Management
Full appointment management, new client scheduling, cancellations, reschedules, and automated reminders so no-shows become rare.
Client Intake & Onboarding
Send, follow up, and manage all intake forms and consent documents. New clients are set up completely before their first session.
Billing & Insurance
Client invoicing, insurance claim submission, payment follow-ups, and denial tracking. You get paid — without chasing anyone.
EHR Management
Data entry, record keeping, and platform management in SimplePractice and ZocDoc. Your charts stay clean and current.
Phone & Email Support
I answer calls, respond to inquiries, and manage your inbox so nothing falls through the cracks — including evenings.
Social Media Management
Instagram and Facebook content creation, scheduling, and engagement — consistent professional presence without you lifting a finger.
