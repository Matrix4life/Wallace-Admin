Services

Everything that pulls you away from your clients.

HIPAA-Compliant Virtual Assistant Services for Mental Health Practices

I handle all the non-clinical operations of your practice — from the first inquiry to the final invoice — so you can stay focused on the people who need you.

Scheduling & Calendar Management
Full appointment management — new client scheduling, cancellations, reschedules, and automated reminders. Your calendar stays clean so no-shows stay rare.
Client Intake & Onboarding
Send, follow up on, and manage all intake forms and consent documents. New clients are fully set up before their first session — without you touching a thing.
Billing & Insurance
Client invoicing, insurance claim submission, payment follow-ups, and denial tracking. You get paid consistently — without chasing a single invoice yourself.
EHR Management
Data entry, record keeping, and platform management in SimplePractice, ZocDoc, and Headway. Your client charts stay accurate, current, and compliant at all times.
Phone & Email Support
I answer calls, respond to client and insurance inquiries, and manage your inbox — including evening hours. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Social Media Management
Instagram and Facebook content creation, scheduling, and engagement — a consistent professional presence for your practice without you lifting a finger.
